In an effort to further improve the Burton Hut roof after its repair this summer, the VOC Huts and Trails committee has installed a webcam livestream!

Powered by Starlink, we wanted a live view of Garibladi Lake so now we know when it's frozen and ready to cross and have fun times at Burton Hut!

The webcam is powered by STOKE: Solar-Thermal Off-grid Kinetic Energy, a new technology that we are deploying in our backcountry huts. We hope to install projectors in the near future so that livestreaming our favourite content creators will be possible!
